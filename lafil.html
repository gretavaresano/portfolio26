La Filarmonica di Milano

LaFil (Filarmonica di Milano) needed to bridge the gap between classical heritage and a younger, digital-native audience. The project translates a traditional institutional identity into a vibrant interface that balances musical rigor with a bold, "pop" spirit.


Rhythmic Structure

The visual language is defined by a "metronomic" use of dashed lines, creating a lightweight grid that guides the user without weighing down the content. This structural discipline is disrupted by rounded shapes and high-contrast color accents, injecting a sense of playfulness into the orchestral world.


Desktop-First Polish

With a focus on the desktop experience, we prioritized high-polish micro-interactions. Every hover state and transition acts as a rhythmic cue, turning a complex concert calendar into a fluid, tactile journey that feels as precise as a live performance.

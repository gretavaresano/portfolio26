NaturaSì

Originally conceived as a tactical "patch" operation, the project evolved into a comprehensive identity and UX overhaul for NaturaSì, Italy's leading organic supermarket. The challenge was to transform a complex e-commerce infrastructure into a seamless, mission-driven ecosystem that bridges the gap between ethical values and digital convenience.


Organic System & Identity

Beyond the logo, we redefined the brand’s digital signature across every touchpoint—from social-first "Stories" to editorial recipe layouts. The visual language balances the warmth of the organic sector with the functional precision of a large-scale retailer, ensuring the brand’s "bio" DNA is felt in every interaction.


Desktop-First Efficiency

Recognizing that a significant portion of grocery shopping still happens via desktop, we obsessed over the transactional flow. We developed a modular component system featuring high-polish UI elements—such as the intuitive delivery-slot hover panel—designed to reduce friction and bring a sense of tactile ease to the digital aisle.


Omnichannel Utility

From a mobile-optimized Store Locator to data-dense product pages, the design ensures a frictionless transition between online shopping and physical stores. Every component was built to be scalable, turning a fragmented digital presence into a unified, high-performance retail experience.

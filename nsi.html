NaturaSì

Challenge

What started as a tactical "patch" evolved into a full-scale UX/UI overhaul of Italy's leading organic retailer. The core objective was to re-engineer a fragmented digital infrastructure into a high-performance, unified system across Web and Mobile App.


Systemic Identity & Design Standards

Beyond the visual rebrand, I architected a modular design language optimized for cross-platform consistency. Using Atomic Design principles, I developed a rigorous, scalable framework that ensures every interaction—from editorial content to transactional components—remains consistent across the entire ecosystem.


High-Intent Transactional Efficiency

Since grocery shopping is a data-heavy activity, I focused on optimizing the transactional flow. I engineered a custom component library featuring high-polish UI elements—such as the delivery-slot management panel—specifically designed to reduce friction and cognitive load during the high-intent checkout process on both desktop and mobile interfaces.


Omnichannel & Mobile Strategy

I led the transition to a unified omnichannel experience, ensuring a seamless flow between the web store and the native mobile application. Even with complex multi-warehouse logistics, I prioritized performance and usability, designing mobile-first features like the integrated Store Locator and synchronized user profiles to bridge the gap between digital and physical retail touchpoints.

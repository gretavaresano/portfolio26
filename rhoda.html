Rhoda

Rhoda is a premium service designed to simplify company formation and visa processes in the UAE. Developed as a strategic design test, the project transforms complex legal requirements into a seamless, high-trust digital journey for global founders.


Visual Contrast & Trust

The identity balances institutional reliability with digital speed. A sophisticated palette of sand-inspired off-whites and deep greens provides a grounded feel, ignited by an acid green accent that signals innovation and efficiency.


Strategic Architecture

A systematic repetition of components and copy reduces cognitive load, building deep user trust within a dense legal environment. This operational flow—from educational content to the "Vault" document management system—is fully optimized for mobile, ensuring founders stay connected to their business setup at all times.

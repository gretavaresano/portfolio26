La DoubleJ x Orlebar Brown

A collaborative seasonal takeover between Orlebar Brown and La DoubleJ, centered on a high-summer capsule collection. The project’s core purpose was to celebrate the "Italianità" through a nostalgic, sun-drenched lens, translating the essence of a Southern Italian summer into a cohesive digital and physical ecosystem.


Nostalgic Signature

The visual direction draws inspiration from vintage Italian signage and mid-century coastal aesthetics. We anchored the palette with a specific sun-faded yellow—balanced between acid and soft—and integrated custom typography inspired by traditional Italian shop signs to evoke an authentic, "Grand Tour" atmosphere.


360° Immersion

From editorial landing pages to localized physical event assets, the project ensured a seamless brand narrative. We curated a rhythmic flow across newsletters and social-first "Stories," focusing on the textures of the swimwear and the warmth of the Mediterranean to turn a product launch into a cultural experience.


Collaborative Polish

As part of a multi-disciplinary effort, the focus remained on elevating the UI through a high-craft approach. Every touchpoint—from digital layouts to on-site event graphics—was designed to feel like a curated summer postcard: fresh, evocative, and deeply rooted in a specific sense of place.
